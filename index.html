About Me

I'm John and I love tech

From a young age I have loved taking things apart and discovering how things tick. Naturally, this led me down the road of technology, science, and engineering. In high school I joined the small but passionate robotics team. This is where I fell in love with everything from the design process, to screwing the last bolt into our robot, and even the hair wrenching debug phase of not adding a semicolon to the end of my C++ class. After high school I began creating projects in Unreal Engine 4, where I am currently working on my own game with a small team of two. In college I took an Intro into Computer Science class creating simple html and css web pages. I found that designing and building websites scratched that creative itch of discovering how things tick. This opened a whole new world of front end web development for me. I started freelancing and building websites for businesses quickly after developing my design principles and constructing pages just for myself. Continuing on my web development path I am constantly trying to push myself into new and exciting technologies. My overall goal is to become a fullstack developer.
